In Loving Memory of Eleana Waide

Eleana Waide lived near the West Lake Landfill and was diagnosed with osteosarcoma on January 7th, 2010. She bravely fought cancer and rang the bell in November 2010. Sadly, the cancer returned and spread to her lungs.

She passed away peacefully at home on April 3, 2014.

I miss you every day, Aunt Ellie.
