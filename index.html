In Loving Memory of Eleana Waide

Eleana Waide lived near the West Lake Landfill and was diagnosed with osteosarcoma on January 7th, 2010. She rang the bell in November 2010, but the cancer later spread to her lungs.

Her family traveled everywhere seeking treatment. Eleana passed away at home on April 3, 2014.
